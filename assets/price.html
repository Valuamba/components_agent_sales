Actuator IC 20-30W3E

  • Running time: 30 seconds
  • Mains Voltage: 230 V AC, -15/+10% 50/60 Hz
  • Torque: 3.0Nm
  • Continuous Control: 0 (4) – 20 mA, 0 – 10 V
  • For basic applications with continuous or three-point step control and automatic/manual mode changeover for easy commissioning
  • Electronic positioning function and adjustable behavior in the event of cable break
  • A position indicator that can be read externally
  • Spacious connection chamber for ease of installation
  • Actuator can be mounted directly onto the butterfly valves BVG or BVH

Applications (click on image for close-up view)

IC 20..E, continuous control

For processes that require high temperature accuracy and low circulation in the furnace. The actuator IC 20..E is controlled by a 4 – 20 mA, 0 – 20 mA or 0 – 10 V signal.
